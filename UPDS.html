ABOUT US

The United Persons with Disabilities (UPWDs) is a community-based organization (CBO) for and by people with disabilities established in 2020 in Bweyogerere parish in Uganda to support people living with disabilities (PWDs) progress to more sustained livelihoods through meaningful engagement in social entrepreneurship. The organization has a vision of empowering a total of 500 people with disabilities to live independent and meaningful lives by January 2027 by offering opportunities for self-employment. Our work is guided by our mission “To empower people with disabilities to contribute to community development by providing them access to employment opportunities.”

OUR OBJECTIVES ARE :

1. To support people with disabilities progress to sustained and meaningful employments through social entrepreneurship.

2. To provide training and tailored mentorship in core business management aspects such as finance, planning and venture creation.

3. To provide capacity for businesses owned and managed by people with disabilities to gain from government Special Grant and business creation schemes such as Social Assistance Grant for Empowerment (SAGE), Uganda Women Entrepreneurship Program (UWEP), Youth Livelihood Fund (YLP), Emyooga, and Parish Development Model (PDM).

OUR THEORY OF CHANGE

a holistic community development is not a one man’s efforts, instead, it arises from many dimensions of influence and empowerment shaped by appropriate and equal distribution of resources, power and voice. Our assumption is that empowering people with disabilities to identify and utilize opportunities in entrepreneurship can achieve both social integration and economic empowerment. This ensures that all people regardless of disabilities are fully engaged in the community development processes, and are valued, and treated with DECORUM.

OUR VISION

A thriving community of empowered individuals with disabilities, driving positive change and progress in their communities

OUR MISSION

Transforming lives by empowering 500 people with disabilities towards selfsufficiency and purposeful living by January 2027

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to position UPWDs as a benchmark for contemporary disability organizations, characterized not by our weaknesses, but by our commitment to empower and support individuals we serve, enabling them to succeed.
